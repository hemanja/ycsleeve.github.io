Yellow Wax Tube

黄腊管

Cost-Effective Insulation Easy to Use

Yellow wax tube (also known as varnished cambric tube) is made of cotton fabric impregnated with insulating varnish. It is an economical insulation solution with good electrical properties and flexibility, commonly used in general electrical applications.

Specifications

MaterialCotton fabric + Insulating varnish
Voltage Rating200V - 1000V
Temperature Range-20°C ~ +105°C
ColorYellow / Natural
FeaturesFlexible, Easy to cut

Applications

  • Motor winding
  • Transformer insulation
  • General wiring
  • Electrical repairs
