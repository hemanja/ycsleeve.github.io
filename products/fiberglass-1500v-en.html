Fiberglass Silicone Sleeve

1500V Rated UL Certified VDE Certified ROHS Compliant

1500V Fiberglass Silicone Sleeve is made of alkali-free fiberglass braided tube coated with high-quality silicone resin. It features excellent heat resistance, insulation properties, and flexibility. Ideal for medium-low voltage electrical equipment insulation protection, motors, transformers, and electrical products.
