About Us Who We Are

Foshan Yingcan Insulation Material Co., Ltd.

Located in Shunde District, Foshan City, Guangdong Province, we are a professional manufacturer dedicated to R&D, production, and sales of insulation sleeves. With 18 years of focus on fiberglass sleeve manufacturing, we maintain strict quality control from raw material selection to finished product delivery.

Our main products include silicone-treated fiberglass sleeves, PVC tubes, heat shrink tubes, silicone tubes, corrugated tubes, and more. Widely used in motors, transformers, electrical appliances, electronics, automotive, and new energy industries.

Products are UL, VDE, ROHS certified and exported to over 50 countries and regions including Europe, America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.
