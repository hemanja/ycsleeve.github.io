Giới Thiệu 关于我们

Since 2008

One Sleeve, Eighteen Years of Craftsmanship

In 2008, we planted the first seed of "insulation" in Shunde, Foshan. At that time, we only had a few braiding machines and a few workers, but we had one obsession - to make the best fiberglass sleeves in China.

For eighteen years, we have witnessed every change in the industry: from traditional motors to new energy vehicles, from Trang Chủ appliances to intelligent robots, from domestic market to more than 50 countries around the world. Every sleeve leaving our Nhà Máy carries our commitment to quality.

Today, Yingcan has grown into a professional insulation material manufacturer integrating R&D, production and sales. Our Sản Phẩm protect BYD's new energy vehicle motors, Midea's appliance cores, Smoore's electronic cigarette cores... We don't just produce sleeves, we protect the safety of electrical current.
